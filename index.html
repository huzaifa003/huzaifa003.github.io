CartwrightKing Ltd
100–150 employee UK legal consultation firm · SRA & LAA regulated
Overview
The Problem

Legal compliance demands pinpoint accuracy. Generic LLMs hallucinate and miss jurisdiction-specific regulations — a critical liability in a regulated industry. CartwrightKing needed an AI solution delivering reliable, SRA and LAA compliant outputs they could stake their reputation on.

The Solution

Built an agentic RAG architecture using SRA/LAA regulatory datasets as the grounded knowledge base. The LLM retrieves context-specific compliance requirements per query — eliminating hallucinations and delivering audit-ready legal guidance with source citations the firm can verify.

Key Highlights
🏛️
SRA & LAA Compliance Knowledge base built from official SRA and LAA regulatory datasets — outputs are regulation-grounded, not guessed.
🔗
Source Citations Every response includes verifiable citations so solicitors can audit and trust AI-generated guidance.
🤖
Agentic RAG Pipeline Multi-step agentic retrieval that dynamically selects and ranks the most relevant regulatory context per query.
⚡
Full Stack Delivery End-to-end ownership — Django REST backend, React SaaS frontend, vector DB, and production deployment.
